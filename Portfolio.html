LEGAL PROBLEMS FOR DIGITAL FORENSICS

When it comes to investigating a criminal case, there are legal problems and challenges that investigators run across. It includes the likes of the Electronic Communication Privacy Act, Intellucatian Property, and rapid changes in technology. With the ECPA, law enforcement agencies needed to require permission from the judge to get a search warrant on the electronic communication (phone calls, emails, computers, etc.). Intellectual Property includes trademarks, patents, copyright issues, or giving out confidential information to a company.

FRYE & DAUBERT

Fyre and Daubert are both standards in a Court of Law. The Frye follows the general acceptance test and acts as a gatekeeper by the scientific community. However, Daubert opposes the Fyre and offers flexibility during a case. In the United States, the majority of them replace the Frye Standard.
